Victoria, the capital city of British Columbia, is an international tourism destination. The seaside city is one of the sunniest spots in the province and offers a full range of first class hotels and restaurants. It's also in close proximity to some of the best fishing in the world.

Victoria is readily accessible by air and sea. Several major air and marine carriers, including ferry systems between Victoria and Vancouver, BC and Washington State, provide frequent service.
